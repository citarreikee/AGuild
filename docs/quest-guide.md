# 📖 Quest Guide · 悬赏指南

## For Quest Givers · 发布者指南

### How to Post a Good Quest · 如何发布好悬赏

A well-written quest gets claimed faster. Follow these principles:

1. **Be specific about the outcome.** "I need a website" is bad. "I need a single-page React app with 3 routes, dark mode, and a contact form" is good.
2. **Set a fair reward.** Check past completed quests to calibrate. Underpriced quests sit unclaimed.
3. **Define clear deliverables.** A checklist in the issue body removes ambiguity.
4. **Be responsive.** Adventurers may have questions before claiming. Quick replies = faster delivery.
5. **Pay on time.** If you say "payment within 24h of completion," honor it. Reputation goes both ways.

### After Posting · 发布之后

- Monitor the issue for `/claim` comments
- Assign the adventurer who claims
- Change the label from `📋 Open` to `⚡ Claimed`
- Stay available for questions during the quest
- When deliverables arrive, review promptly
- On approval: close the issue, change label to `✅ Completed`

## For Adventurers · 冒险家指南

### How to Claim a Quest · 如何认领悬赏

1. Browse [Open Quests](https://github.com/citarreikee/AGuild/issues?q=label%3A%22%F0%9F%93%8B+Open%22)
2. Read the quest carefully. Make sure you can deliver.
3. Comment `/claim` with a brief note on your approach:
   ```
   /claim
   
   I can take this. My approach:
   1. [Step one]
   2. [Step two]
   3. Deliver within [timeline]
   
   Relevant work: [link to similar past work if any]
   ```
4. Wait for the Quest Giver to assign you
5. Once assigned, begin work

### Delivering · 交付

- Open a **Pull Request** linking the quest issue
- Or link your external delivery (deployed URL, Google Doc, Figma file, etc.) in the issue comments
- Request review from the Quest Giver
- Address feedback promptly (within the agreed revision rounds)

### Getting Paid · 获取报酬

- Payment methods are negotiated in the issue
- For first-time collaborations, consider milestone-based payment (e.g., 50% upfront, 50% on delivery)
- If a Quest Giver fails to pay, report in Discussions — the Guild tracks deadbeat posters

## Quest Etiquette · 悬赏礼仪

| Do ✅ | Don't ❌ |
|-------|---------|
| Be specific about scope | Ghost after claiming a quest |
| Communicate delays early | Claim quests you can't deliver |
| Respect the agreed timeline | Spam `/claim` on every quest |
| Leave constructive feedback | Disappear after getting paid |

---

## Dispute Resolution · 争议处理

1. **Discuss first.** Most issues are misunderstandings. Talk it out in the issue thread.
2. **Call a Guild Moderator.** If you can't resolve it, tag a guild moderator in the thread.
3. **Public record.** The GitHub issue serves as the public record. Keep all communication there.

In the future, the Guild DAO will handle formal arbitration.
