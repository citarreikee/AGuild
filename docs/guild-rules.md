# 🛡️ Guild Rules · 公会守则

## The Guild Charter · 公会宪章

The Adventurer's Guild exists to create a fair, transparent marketplace for AI-augmented work. Every member — Quest Giver and Adventurer alike — agrees to these rules.

冒险家公会为 AI 赋能的工作创造一个公平透明的市场。每位成员——无论是悬赏发布者还是冒险家——都同意遵守以下规则。

---

## Core Principles · 核心原则

### 1. Merit Above All · 能力至上

Quests go to those who can deliver, not those with the best credentials. Your work speaks. Nothing else.

### 2. Fair Compensation · 公平报酬

AI-augmented workers produce disproportionate value. Pay for results, not hours. A 2-hour quest that took 10 years of experience to execute is worth its reward.

### 3. Transparent Process · 透明过程

All quest communication stays in the open (issue comments). No secret DMs, no side deals. The public record protects both parties.

### 4. Guild Before Self · 公会优先于个人

Protect the Guild's reputation. If you make a mistake, own it. If you see bad behavior, flag it. The Guild is only as strong as its members' integrity.

---

## Rules for Quest Givers · 悬赏发布者规则

| # | Rule |
|---|------|
| G1 | Pay what you promised, when you promised |
| G2 | Don't cancel a quest after work has begun without compensation |
| G3 | Review deliverables within 5 business days |
| G4 | Provide constructive feedback — "not good enough" is not feedback |
| G5 | Don't use the Guild to source free speculative work |

## Rules for Adventurers · 冒险家规则

| # | Rule |
|---|------|
| A1 | Only `/claim` quests you can realistically complete |
| A2 | Deliver what was agreed. Scope creep requires renegotiation |
| A3 | Communicate delays immediately — silence is the worst response |
| A4 | Don't use AI-generated work without disclosure — AI is a tool, not a substitute for quality |
| A5 | Don't poach Quest Givers from other Adventurers mid-quest |

---

## Prohibited Quests · 禁止的悬赏

The following quest types are not allowed:

- ❌ Illegal activities in any jurisdiction
- ❌ Academic fraud (writing theses, taking exams for others)
- ❌ Generating disinformation, spam, or malware
- ❌ Anything that violates GitHub's [Terms of Service](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/github-terms/github-terms-of-service)

---

## Consequences · 后果

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|------------|
| First minor infraction | Warning in-thread |
| Repeated issues | Temporary ban from posting/claiming |
| Severe violation (fraud, non-payment) | Permanent guild ban + public notice |
