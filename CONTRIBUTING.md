# Join the Guild

## Post a Quest

[Create a quest issue](https://github.com/citarreikee/AGuild/issues/new?template=quest.yml). Describe what needs doing and what you'll pay.

## Claim a Quest

Browse the [Quest Board](https://citarreikee.github.io/AGuild) or [open quests](https://github.com/citarreikee/AGuild/issues?q=label%3A%22%F0%9F%9F%A2+Open%22). Comment on the issue to claim it.

## Get Paid

Payment happens directly between Quest Giver and Adventurer. Discuss method and terms in the issue thread.
