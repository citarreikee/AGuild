# ⚔️ Join the Guild · 加入公会

Welcome, adventurer. Here's how to join.

欢迎，冒险家。以下是加入方式。

---

## Step 1: Introduce Yourself · 介绍自己

Head to [Discussions → Introductions](https://github.com/citarreikee/AGuild/discussions) and tell us:

- Who you are
- What skills you bring
- What kind of quests you're looking for
- Your timezone / availability

## Step 2: Browse the Quest Board · 浏览悬赏看板

Check the [Quest Board](https://citarreikee.github.io/AGuild) or [Open Quests](https://github.com/citarreikee/AGuild/issues?q=label%3A%22%F0%9F%93%8B+Open%22). Find something that matches your skills.

## Step 3: Claim Your First Quest · 认领你的第一个任务

Comment `/claim` on a quest issue. Be specific about your approach. Once assigned, deliver excellent work.

## Step 4: Build Your Reputation · 积累声望

Complete quests → earn rank → unlock higher-tier quests → repeat.

---

## Ways to Contribute · 贡献方式

| You can… | By… |
|----------|-----|
| ⚔️ Complete quests | Claiming and delivering bounties |
| 📜 Post quests | Creating bounties for work you need |
| 🛡️ Improve the Guild | PRs to this repo (docs, site, workflows) |
| 🏰 Moderate | Help resolve disputes, verify ranks |
| 📣 Spread the word | Share the Guild with skilled AI workers |

---

## Guild Values · 公会价值观

- **Excellence over credentials.** Deliver great work, regardless of background.
- **Speed + quality.** AI is an accelerant — use it.
- **Direct and honest.** Say what you mean. Flag problems early.
- **Pay it forward.** Mentor novices. Strengthen the guild.
